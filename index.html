About Us
Welcome to our website! We are a group of four individuals who have worked together to create this website as a part of our project for 'Skillabhudaya', an event conducted by the 'Force Club' of IIIT Una.

We are a team of four people among which three of us, Amrutansh Jha, Lakshay Dhall and Mayank Sharma have worked together to make this website. The fourth member is Vaibhav Sharma who is also our mentor and senior and has helped us alot with his experience in this field to guide us to make and improve the website.

Together, we have put in alot of hardwork to create a platform that is made to make the library experience as smooth and stress-free as possible for students. We are committed to providing our users with accurate and up-to-date information regarding the availability of books in library.

As a team, we are constantly learning and striving to improve our skills. We are excited to share our work and use the experience we have gained for future implications!

Thank you for visiting our website, and we hope you find it useful as well as helpful.
